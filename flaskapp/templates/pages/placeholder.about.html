The Coronavirus Tweets Text Mining and Sentiment Prediction

This is a final project for BIS 634, Computational Methods for Informatics, supervised by Prof. Robert Mcdougal

COVID - 19

Coronavirus disease 2019 (COVID-19) is a contagious disease caused by severe acute respiratory syndrome coronavirus 2 (SARS-CoV-2). It has spread worldwide, leading to an ongoing pandemic since Dec 2019.

Social Media

Since there wasn’t any proved effective medicine or vaccine back then, CDC initiated a quarantine guideline for COVID-19 prevention. Most of the schooling and work were moved online until now. Social Media became an essential resources for people to communicate with each other. It certainly made our lives convenient. However, it's a double-edged sword. Misinformation, fake new and extreme emotion can mislead the public. In a way, remote work and learning increased the anxiety in public because of the social media.

Study Objective

One of largest online public communities Twitter is an important resource for people to learn about this new virus and communicate with each other. This study focused on the Coronavirus Tweets Text Mining and Sentiment analysis. It's intended to be a public visualization tool for public to learn about the topics and people's emotion during COVID-19.

Data Resources

I am using it’s a coronavirus tweets text data fromkaggle public data Repositories. Seethe data source here Coronavirus tweets NLP - Text Classification. It was collected fromTwitter streamwith a purpose of scientific use and all the confidential information was removed from the dataset.
